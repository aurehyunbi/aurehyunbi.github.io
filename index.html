Hi, this is a website I made to showcase my stickers sheet. I don't really know how to do a real shopping website so for any inquiry please contact me either on social medias or by email.

Prices can vary with the number of sheets you want. If you take more than one it's less expensive to produce and send so the cost goes down.

All of these are made entirely by me from drawing to the final result. I draw them, print them, and cut them (with a machine) and I'll send them to you if you want some.
